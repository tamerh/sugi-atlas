### Pharmacogenomics
- **VIP Gene Status**: Yes (PharmGKB PA24684)
- **CPIC Guideline**: None available
- **Germline Variants** (ClinVar): 100+ variants catalogued; predominantly benign/likely benign with ~25 of uncertain significance
- **Clinical Relevance**: Primary focus is **tumor somatic mutations** (PIK3CA, AKT1, PTEN) driving AKT pathway activation, not germline pharmacogenomics
- **Dosing Guidelines**: No published CPIC/FDA guidance; trial-specific dosing used (capivasertib: 400mg BID on 4-days-on/3-days-off schedule)
- **Key Predictive Markers**: PIK3CA mutations, PTEN loss, AKT mutations enhance drug efficacy
