The gene KRAS (HGNC:6407) is located at 12p12.1 and has the approved symbol KRAS and aliases KRAS1 and K-Ras4B. It has 16 Ensembl transcripts, 22 RefSeq mRNAs, and a canonical transcript (ENST00000311936) with 5 exons. The canonical reviewed UniProt accession is P01116, with 9 additional UniProt accessions, and 15 RefSeq protein entries. KRAS has 462 experimental structures in the PDB, primarily determined by X-ray diffraction, and a predicted AlphaFold model (AF-P01116-F1) with a pLDDT score of 91.65. The gene has 3 cross-species orthologs and 35 paralogs, including RALA and RAP1A. ClinVar reports 565 variants, with 37 classified as pathogenic and 37 as likely pathogenic.
