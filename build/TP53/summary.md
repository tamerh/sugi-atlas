The gene TP53, approved symbol TP53 and aliases p53 and LFS1, is located at 17p13.1 and has the HGNC ID HGNC:11998, OMIM ID 191170, and Entrez ID 7157. It has 39 Ensembl transcripts, 46 RefSeq mRNAs including MANE Select NM_000546, and 11 exons in its canonical transcript ENST00000269305. The canonical protein is UniProt accession P04637, with 34 RefSeq protein accessions and 19 total UniProt accessions. TP53 has 297 experimental PDB structures, a predicted AlphaFold model AF-P04637-F1 with pLDDT 75.83, and 1518 UniProt features including 1363 sequence variants. It has 4 cross-species orthologs in danio_rerio, mus_musculus, rattus_norvegicus, and drosophila_melanogaster, and 2 paralogs TP63 and TP73. ClinVar reports 3850 variants with 749 pathogenic and 199 likely pathogenic classifications.
