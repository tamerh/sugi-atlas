The gene TP53, approved symbol TP53 and aliases p53 and LFS1, is located at 17p13.1 and has the HGNC ID HGNC:11998, OMIM ID 191170, and Entrez ID 7157. It has 39 Ensembl transcripts, 46 RefSeq mRNAs, and a canonical transcript ENST00000269305 with 11 exons. The canonical reviewed UniProt accession is P04637, with 18 additional UniProt accessions, and 34 RefSeq proteins, including NP_000537. TP53 has 297 experimental PDB structures and 4 cross-species orthologs in danio_rerio, mus_musculus, rattus_norvegicus, and drosophila_melanogaster, along with 2 paralogs, TP63 and TP73. It is associated with 3850 ClinVar variants, including 749 pathogenic and 199 likely pathogenic, and is linked to 75 Mondo diseases such as Li-Fraumeni syndrome and various cancers.
