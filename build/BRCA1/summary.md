BRCA1, approved symbol BRCA1 and gene name BRCA1 DNA repair associated, is located at 17q21.31 and has HGNC ID HGNC:1100, OMIM ID 113705, and Entrez ID 672. It has aliases RNF53, BRCC1, PPP1R53, and FANCS, and its Ensembl gene ID is ENSG00000012048 with a protein_coding biotype. The gene has 47 Ensembl transcripts, including protein_coding, retained_intron, and nonsense_mediated_decay types, and 371 RefSeq mRNAs with MANE Select transcript NM_007294. The canonical transcript ENST00000357654 contains 23 exons, and the canonical reviewed UniProt protein is P38398, with 22 total UniProt accessions. The gene is associated with 15445 ClinVar variants, including 4004 pathogenic and 322 likely pathogenic variants, and has 33 experimental PDB structures and one AlphaFold predicted model.
