CDKN2A, located at 9p21.3, has HGNC ID 1787, OMIM ID 600160, and Entrez ID 1029, with aliases including CDK4I, p16, INK4a, MTS1, ARF, p19, p14, and others. The gene produces 14 Ensembl transcripts and 21 RefSeq mRNAs, with canonical transcript ENST00000304494 containing 3 exons. It encodes proteins with UniProt accessions P42771 and Q8N726 among others, featuring domains such as ankyrin repeats and tumor suppressor ARF. The gene has 1594 ClinVar variants, including at least 150 pathogenic or likely pathogenic variants, and is associated with 224 clinical trials for related diseases. CDKN2A has orthologs in Mus musculus (Cdkn2a) and Rattus norvegicus (Cdkn2a), one paralog CDKN2B, and is expressed ubiquitously across tissues according to Bgee and FANTOM5 data.
